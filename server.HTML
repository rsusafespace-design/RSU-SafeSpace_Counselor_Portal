Your Privacy Matters

Last updated: October 15, 2023

SafeSpace respects your right to privacy and is committed to protecting the confidentiality of your personal and mental health information. This policy explains how your data is collected, processed, and protected while using the Student Portal.

🔐
Data Secured
Your counseling data is transmitted and stored through Firebase's encrypted infrastructure, ensuring confidentiality and protection.
👁️
You're In Control
You may manage your account, review stored information, and request correction or deletion of your data in line with institutional policy.
📋
Compliant
SafeSpace follows the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173) and Google's Privacy and Security Principles, ensuring that sensitive counseling records are handled with care and transparency.
🧍‍♂️

Data We Collect

When you use the SafeSpace Student Portal, we collect and process the following information:

  • • Personal information such as Full Name, Student ID, and Email/Username
  • • Counseling messages and session details for mental health support
  • • Uploaded test results or records related to counseling sessions
  • • System-related data such as login activity and usage timestamps for security tracking

All collected information is used solely for counseling, academic, and administrative purposes in line with institutional policies.

🔐

Data Protection and Security

How we protect your sensitive information

  • ✓ All student information is securely stored in Firebase's encrypted database, ensuring confidentiality and integrity.
  • ✓ Data is encrypted in transit and at rest to prevent unauthorized access.
  • ✓ Only authorized counselors and administrators can access your data for legitimate counseling and support purposes.
  • ✓ Automatic logout and authentication mechanisms protect your account when inactive.

SafeSpace follows Google's security standards, applying secure cloud storage and strict access controls.

💬

Counseling Messages & Results

Confidentiality of your mental health information

All counseling conversations and uploaded results are strictly confidential.

These records are accessible only to the assigned counselor and authorized mental health staff.

No counseling information is shared publicly or with third parties without your consent or legal authorization.

👁️

Your Rights Under RA 10173

Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012

Under the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173), students have the right to:

Access Information
Access their personal information stored in the system
Request Correction
Request correction of any inaccurate or outdated data
Withdraw Consent
Withdraw consent for data processing, subject to university policies
File Complaints
File complaints or concerns to the Data Protection Officer (DPO) or the National Privacy Commission (NPC)

To exercise these rights, you may contact the university's DPO or guidance office.

📋

Data Retention and Deletion

How long we keep your information

Student counseling data is retained only for the duration necessary to fulfill academic and mental health purposes.

After this period, data is securely deleted or anonymized according to institutional retention policies.

Accountability and Contact

Who to contact for privacy concerns

All student data collected through SafeSpace is managed under the supervision of the Data Protection Officer (DPO) and system administrator.

For privacy-related requests or questions, you may contact the designated DPO through official university channels.
